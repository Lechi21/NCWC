About Us

The National Creative Writer's Organization is co-founded by Rasing Young Authors and the National Planning Partner (BirchHill Consulting).
It is established to encourage young children and to ignite the passion for writing through cutting edge methodologies.
Equipping teachers with the engaging, interactive and fun strategies that makes creative writing easy.
